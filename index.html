MS Applied Data Science, USC Viterbi School of Engineering

I am a graduate student at the University of Southern California pursuing a Masters of Science in Applied Data Science. I have a background in data analytics as it relates to investment research, investment management, and credit risk management. Prior to pursuing my masters, I was employed at Wasatch Global Investors. During my time at Wasatch, I supported portfolio managers in developing data-driven market insights and locating favorable opportunities for Wasatch’s 17 actively managed strategies. Naive Bayes Hotel Review Classifier

I wrote a Naive Bayes classifier from scratch (without the use of any libraries) to classify hotel reviews by two separate labels. The first label classifies the reviews as having either positive or negative sentiment and the second classifies the reviews as being either truthful or deceptive. This classifier returned a mean F1 score of 0.88.

Please find the specific GitHub Repository which includes the README, source code, and data for this project here.
